A precise and professional compass and location tracking tool that utilizes multi-source data from networks, GPS, and mobile sensors to provide high-accuracy geographic location information. It not only helps you quickly determine direction but also displays real-time data such as latitude and longitude, altitude, magnetic field strength, atmospheric pressure, and more. Ideal for outdoor adventures, photography, and surveying.

Features:
- Quickly obtain latitude, longitude, altitude, real-time atmospheric pressure, magnetic field intensity, and other environmental data.
- Detailed recording of altitude data with trend charts for daily altitude changes.
- GPS tracking of visited locations, displayed as a heatmap.
- Overlay compass, latitude, and longitude information in camera mode for easy location recording and sharing.
- Bar charts displaying daily step count and distance.
- View sunrise and sunset times, moon phases, and boiling point of water for your current location.

Key Highlights:
- Track Map: High-precision map display for intuitive viewing of your location and surroundings.
- Comprehensive Route Recording: Logs all outdoor activity routes for easy review and analysis.
- Altitude Tracking: Records daily altitude changes and tracks the highest and lowest altitudes reached.
- Offline Functionality: Compass works even without network or GPS signals.

Whether you’re hiking, camping, surveying, or photographing, this professional compass and location tool provides accurate navigation and real-time monitoring to meet the needs of outdoor professionals.
